About Rajendra

Where numbers meet human possibility.

“Every student deserves to experience mathematics as something they can understand, question, and use.”

Based in Baltimore, I support K–12 and college learners through student-centered, technology-integrated, and inclusive mathematics instruction. My research explores mathematics anxiety, artificial intelligence in education, STEM equity, and data-informed educational practice.

Outside academia, I create Nepali music and cultural stories as Yatri Dai—work that keeps me connected to identity, community, and home.
