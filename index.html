01About

Mathematics becomes powerful when every learner feels they belong.

I am a Nepali-American mathematics educator and researcher based in Baltimore, Maryland. My work brings together student-centered teaching, educational technology, and evidence-based research.

From teaching in Nepal to supporting learners and conducting research at Morgan State University, my purpose is to make STEM learning more inclusive, human, and hopeful.
